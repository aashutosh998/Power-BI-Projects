# Power-BI-Projects
Power BI Projects - 
The Power BI Projects repository is a collection of interactive Power BI dashboards and analytical projects designed to demonstrate data visualization, reporting, and business intelligence skills. It includes multiple real-world business scenarios such as Music Store, Sales Analysis, End-to-End Business Performance, Pizza Sales, and Restaurant Ratings. Each project focuses on transforming raw data into meaningful insights through data modeling, DAX calculations, KPIs, charts, and dashboards.
Projects are following :
a). Music Store
b). Sales Analysis
c). End-to-End Business Performance
d). Pizza Sales
e). Restaurant Ratings
